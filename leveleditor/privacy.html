Privacy Policy

Please read this privacy policy to know what data is being collected through your use of Level Editor, how it is being stored and processed and why.

Analytics

To improve the software, Unity analytics is used. All data collected is anonymized and I have no way to track it back to an individual. Data collected may include (not an exhaustive list): number of players, sessions, retention, levels created, levels deleted etc. This data isn't sold, it's so I can get insights into how the game is being played, so I can improve it in the future. Anonymized, non-personal data may be publicly displayed, such as showing the number of levels created on the internet. You can opt out of analytics in settings. Read the Unity privacy policy at https://unity3d.com/legal/privacy-policy

Changes

This privacy policy may be changed at any time. Advance notice of one (1) month must be given before changes go into effect.
