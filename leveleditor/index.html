Donutask

A game where you make your own games

Using simple tools you can create almost anything. Quickly play your levels, then tweak them however you like.

Features

There are 100 blocks, obstacles and other goodies including:

  • Mobile enemies - turtles, big turtles, fire turtles, robot turtles!
  • A variety of power ups.
  • Ladders, springs and fans.
  • Dynamite & cannons.
  • Buttons and toggle blocks for special logic.
  • Different types of solid blocks - grass, stone, bricks, sand, etc.
  • Crates & heavier crates.
  • Grass, flowers, bushes, cacti.
  • Wormholes. Teleport anywhere!
  • Timers, heaters, magnets.
  • Colorful wool blocks.
  • Fireworks!
  • And more to come...

Tools to make things easier. Select tool for filling, moving and cutting large areas and the eyedropper to copy & paste a single block swiftly.

Rotate almost any block, change most timers, and customise cannons. You're in control.

Layers: Add blocks to the background, in the middle or in front. Hide traps behind what looks like solid ground...

Worlds. Play an assortment of levels back to back.

Customise your character with skins, tools and hats.

More awesomeness to come in the future.
