Hi, I'm Charles and I make games.

I do all of it: coding, drawing, debugging, rebuggingm distributing, marketing and more.

Eventually, I published my first app Bread Dash, an android app on Google Play. This app has a lot of bugs from how bad my early code was and I haven't gotten arround to despaghettifying it.

The next published project was Bubble Wrap which was much less complex and easier to work on.

I am currently working on Level Editor a game creation tool of sorts. This is my most complex project yet.
