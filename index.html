About

Game dev from 10 years old.

I started making games in 2016 in Scratch, at primary school. I got better at coding and used Unity to create Bread Dash. Although it was low quality and buggy, I learnt valuable skills which I used for my next projects. Bubble Wrap was my next app and the most successful - with over 28 thousand total users. My largest and most complicated game is Level Editor, a game creation game. I am currently creating YouTube videos.
